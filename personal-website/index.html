Trains

Although this isn't so much a hobby more an interest it's one of the only things I could come up with.
My interest cycle over the course of a year but this is one of my current ones.
The image shown is a picture of Union Pacific 4014.
4014 is a 4-8-8-4 locomotive meaning it has 4 leading and trailing wheels as well as 2 sets of 8 driving wheels.
The wheels are articulated to allow the massive locomotive to go around turns more easily.
4014 and other locomotives of it's type were used in the Wasatch Grade.
It was made to replace the 4-6-6-4 Challenger type locomotives also used in the Wasatch Grade.
A replacement was needed as Challengers would require the help of an aditional locomotive along a certain part of the track.
